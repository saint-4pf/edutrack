Getting Started Guide
1
Configure Term Settings
Go to Term Settings — enter school info, session, term, number on roll, attendance data, and important dates before anything else.
2
Add Subjects
Go to Subjects — add all subjects taught. Each subject is unique per teacher account.
3
Add Students
Go to Students — register each student with their Name, Student ID, and Class.
4
Enter Scores
Go to Enter Scores — search or select a student and subject. Enter SBA (max 50%) and Exam scores. The system calculates the total automatically.
5
View Mastersheet

6
Generate Reports
Go to Reports — search for any student to view and print their official report card with all details.

Subjects

Manage subjects assigned to your account.

💡 Note: Subjects are unique to your teacher account. No two subjects with the same name can be created by the same teacher. Subjects appear as columns in the Mastersheet and rows on report cards.

📌 Guidelines
  • Subject names are unique per teacher — no duplicates allowed.
  • Use consistent, full names: English Language, not Eng.
  • Short codes appear in compact views like the Mastersheet.
  • You can edit or delete subjects that have no scores yet.
  • Subjects with entered scores are locked from deletion to preserve data.
